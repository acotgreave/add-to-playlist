ⓘ About this page
Add to Playlist is one of BBC Radio 4's most quietly wonderful programmes. Each week, two guests join presenters Jeffrey Boakye and Anna Phoebe to add five tracks to an imaginary communal playlist — music they love, music that deserves to be heard. The result is a gloriously eclectic archive of discovery. This is a fan-made explorer of every track ever played. Browse, search, and listen.
